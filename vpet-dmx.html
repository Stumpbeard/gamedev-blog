Features

  • Partner has stats
    • HUNGRY
    • STRENGTH
    • EFFORT
    • AGE
    • LEVEL
    • CARE MISTAKES
    • POWER
    • HP
    • ATTACK
    • STAGE
    • NAME
    • ATTRIBUTE
    • INJURIES
    • WEIGHT
  • There is a way to see Partner's stats
  • Partner has needs that deplete over time (HUNGRY, STRENGTH)
  • You can feed your partner food to bring back HUNGRY, or vitamins to bring back STRENGTH
  • You can train your partner via mini game to raise EFFORT, restore STRENGTH, and lower WEIGHT
  • Can fight other digimon for experience to level up
    • Enemy digimon shows up
    • Partner digimon does series of attacks based on results in minigame + ATTACK
    • Chance to hit based on difference in POWER
    • 2 digimon trade hits until one is out of HP or 5 attacks have launched from both
  • Leveling up changes your ATTACK, POWER, and HP
  • Partner can evolve based on how they're cared for
  • Partner goes into alarm when a meter is empty, accumulates a CARE MISTAKE if not addressed
  • Partner dies and leaves an egg if too many injuries or care mistakes are accumulated, or not addressed for too long
  • Can fight multiple enemies in a row, and upon winning, unlocks new digivolutions, next challenge, and prizes
  • Can feed special foods that give short term bonuses to combat or meter depletion
  • Can roll XAI dice
  • Can cure injuries
  • Partner poops periodically
  • You can clean the poop
  • Too much poop results in illness
  • Partner needs sleep periodicaly
  • Can turn off and on the lights
  • Can view a library of all Digimon achieved
